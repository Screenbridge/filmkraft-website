NYC Video Production FAQs

Do I need a permit to film in NYC?

Most commercial filming in NYC requires a permit from the Mayor's Office of Media & Entertainment (MOME). This includes filming on streets, sidewalks, parks, and most public spaces. Filmkraft handles all permit applications and ensures compliance with NYC filming regulations.

What are the best filming locations in NYC?

NYC offers incredible diversity: Brooklyn brownstones, Manhattan skyscrapers, Central Park, industrial spaces in Long Island City, waterfront locations, and countless unique neighborhoods. Filmkraft has extensive location knowledge and relationships with location scouts across all five boroughs.

How much does video production cost in NYC?

NYC video production costs range from $15,000 for simple projects to $150,000+ for high-end commercials. Costs are typically 20-30% higher than other markets due to crew rates, permits, and location fees. Filmkraft provides transparent pricing and works within various budget ranges.

Can you arrange crew and equipment in NYC?

Yes. Filmkraft has a network of experienced NYC crew members and relationships with top equipment rental houses. We handle all crew hiring, equipment rental, and logistics for seamless NYC productions.

Do you work with union crews?

Yes. NYC is a strong union market and we have experience with SAG-AFTRA talent, IATSE crews, and other unions. We can produce union or non-union depending on your project requirements and budget.

How long does NYC permitting take?

Film permit applications should be submitted at least 5-7 business days before your shoot date. For complex locations or large productions, we recommend 2-3 weeks lead time. Filmkraft handles all applications and follow-up.
