CTV Creative Best Practices

‚ö° Hook in First 3 Seconds

Grab attention immediately with a compelling visual or question. CTV viewers decide whether to pay attention within the first few frames.

üì± Clear, Simple Messaging

One core message per ad. Viewers are watching from their couch, not closely reading fine print. Keep it simple and memorable.

üó£Ô∏è Strong Call-to-Action

Make your CTA prominent and easy to remember: website URL, QR code, or simple action. Repeat it at least twice.

üì∫ Big, Bold Graphics

Text and graphics should be large enough to read on a TV from 10 feet away. Avoid small text, cluttered layouts, and mobile-first design.

üîä Audio-Forward

Unlike social media, CTV ads are almost always watched with sound on. Use voiceover, music, and sound design to reinforce your message.

üéØ Problem-Solution Framework

Quickly establish the problem your product solves, then showcase your solution. Direct response principles work exceptionally well on CTV.

CTV Advertising FAQs

How much does CTV advertising cost?

Production costs for CTV ads typically range from $10,000-$75,000 depending on complexity. Media costs vary widely by targeting and platform but CPMs generally range from $15-$50, making it affordable for most budgets.

What's the difference between CTV and OTT?

CTV (Connected TV) refers specifically to TVs connected to the internet (smart TVs, Roku, Fire TV). OTT (Over-The-Top) is broader and includes any streaming video content delivered via the internet, including mobile and desktop.

What ad lengths work best for CTV?

:15 second ads are most cost-effective and have high completion rates. :30 second ads allow more storytelling. :60 second ads work well for complex products or high-consideration purchases. We recommend creating all three.

Can I use my existing TV commercials on CTV?

Sometimes, but CTV often requires different creative approaches. Traditional broadcast ads may need adaptation: stronger hooks, clearer CTAs, and optimization for performance-driven environments. We can evaluate your existing assets.

How do I buy CTV advertising?

CTV ads are typically purchased through demand-side platforms (DSPs), directly with streaming services, or through programmatic marketplaces. We can guide you through the buying process and recommend media partners.

What results can I expect from CTV advertising?

Typical benchmarks include 70-95% completion rates, 0.5-2% click-through rates, and strong brand lift metrics. Direct response campaigns often see measurable website traffic and conversion increases. Results vary by industry and creative quality.
