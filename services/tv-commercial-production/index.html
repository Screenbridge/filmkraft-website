TV Commercial Production FAQs

How much does TV commercial production cost in NYC?

TV commercial production costs in NYC typically range from $15,000 for simple productions to $150,000+ for high-end campaigns. Factors include talent, locations, production days, and post-production complexity. Filmkraft offers transparent pricing and works within various budget ranges.

Read full cost breakdown ‚Üí

How long does it take to produce a TV commercial?

Typical timeline is 4-8 weeks from concept to final delivery. This includes: 1-2 weeks for creative development and pre-production, 1-3 days for filming, and 2-4 weeks for post-production. Rush timelines are available for time-sensitive campaigns.

What deliverables do I receive?

Standard deliverables include broadcast-ready files (:15s, :30s, :60s), web versions, social media cutdowns, closed captions, and all raw footage. We deliver in formats optimized for TV broadcast, CTV, YouTube, and social platforms.

Do you handle all aspects of production?

Yes. Filmkraft manages the entire production process including: scriptwriting, casting, location scouting, permits, crew, equipment, catering, post-production, music licensing, and final delivery. You get a single point of contact throughout.

Can you produce commercials outside of NYC?

Absolutely. While we're based in NYC, we have a network of crew and production partners nationwide. We regularly produce commercials across the US and can travel to your location or arrange remote production.

Do you offer direct response TV (DRTV) expertise?

Yes! Direct response is our specialty. We understand response-driven creative, call-to-action placement, offer presentation, and performance metrics. Many of our commercials are optimized for measurable response and conversion.
