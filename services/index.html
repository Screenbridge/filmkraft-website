How We Work

A proven production process that delivers results

1

Discovery

We learn about your brand, audience, goals, and budget. Understanding your business ensures our creative aligns with your strategy.

2

Concept

Our creative team develops multiple concept directions with scripts and visual references. You choose the direction that resonates. Once you sign the contract, we progress through thought starters, concepts, scripts, and animatics.

3

Production

We handle all logistics: casting, locations, crew, equipment, and permits. You approve key decisions and we manage the rest. We can shoot in NY or anywhere in the US.

4

Post-Production

Expert editing, color grading, motion graphics, and sound design. Multiple revision rounds ensure you're thrilled with the result.

5

Delivery

Final files delivered in all required formats with technical specs for each platform. Plus raw footage and project files. Typical project timeline is 4-8 weeks from contract signing to final delivery.

Optimization
