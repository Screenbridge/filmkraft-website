üìä Specific Results

Numbers and metrics matter. "Increased sales by 47%" beats "saw great results." Specificity creates credibility and helps prospects visualize their own success.

üé≠ Emotional Authenticity

Real emotion resonates. We don't script testimonials‚Äîwe guide customers to share genuine experiences and feelings. Authenticity builds trust more than polish.

üéØ Address Key Objections

The best testimonials overcome specific hesitations: "I worried about the price, but the ROI was clear within 2 months." Pre-emptively answer common concerns.

üë• Relatable Characters

Feature customers your prospects can identify with. Similar industries, company sizes, roles, or demographics make the testimonial feel relevant and achievable.

üìñ Story Structure

Testimonial Video FAQs

How much do testimonial videos cost?

Single testimonial videos typically range from $3,000-$8,000. Multi-testimonial shoots (filming 3-5 customers in one day) offer better value at $10,000-$25,000. Costs vary based on location, travel, and production complexity.

How do I get customers to participate?

We help with customer recruitment and outreach. Most happy customers are willing to participate, especially if you offer incentives (discounts, gift cards, or exclusive access). We handle all coordination and make the process easy for them.

Should we film on-location or in a studio?

It depends on your goals. On-location filming (customer's office/home) feels more authentic and provides relevant B-roll. Studio filming offers controlled lighting and is more efficient for multiple testimonials in one day. We can advise based on your specific needs.

What if my customer isn't comfortable on camera?

We're experts at coaching camera-shy subjects. Our pre-interview process helps them prepare, and our directors know how to create a comfortable environment. We'll conduct multiple takes and edit for their best moments. Most people are more natural than they expect.

How long should a testimonial video be?

It depends on use case: 15-30 seconds for social ads, 60-90 seconds for website landing pages, 2-5 minutes for B2B case studies. We recommend creating multiple lengths from one shoot to maximize versatility.

Can you help us find customers to feature?

Yes! We can review your customer base and identify strong candidates based on their results, demographics, and articulation. We'll handle outreach, pre-interviews, and qualification to ensure we capture compelling stories.
