E-Commerce Photography Best Practices

üìè Multiple Angles

Show front, back, sides, top, and bottom views. Customers can't touch products online, so comprehensive angles reduce uncertainty and build confidence.

üîç Detail Shots

Capture texture, stitching, materials, and craftsmanship up close. These details differentiate quality products and justify premium pricing.

üìê Scale & Context

Include photos showing size relative to familiar objects or in-use scenarios. Reduces returns from size/scale misunderstandings.

‚ö™ Clean Backgrounds

White backgrounds are standard for product pages and marketplaces. They're professional, clean, and put all focus on the product itself.

üé® Lifestyle Context

At least one lifestyle shot showing the product in use. Helps customers visualize the product in their own lives and creates emotional connection.

üì± Mobile Optimization

Most e-commerce traffic is mobile. Images must look great at small sizes with key details visible even on smartphones.

Photography FAQs

How much does product photography cost?

Professional product photography ranges from $100-$500 per image depending on complexity and retouching. Most e-commerce brands budget $1,500-$5,000 for a shoot covering multiple products. Lifestyle photography with models costs more ($5,000-$15,000+) due to additional production requirements.

How many products can you shoot in one day?

In a studio environment with white backgrounds, we can photograph 15-30 products per day depending on size and complexity. Lifestyle shoots are slower‚Äîtypically 3-8 products per day due to styling, model direction, and setup changes.

Do you provide models and styling?

Yes! We handle model casting, wardrobe styling, prop sourcing, and set design. We'll work with your budget and creative direction to build the right team for your shoot.

Where do the shoots take place?

We have access to professional studios in NYC for controlled product photography. For lifestyle shoots, we can work on-location (homes, offices, outdoor locations) or in lifestyle-friendly studio spaces. Location depends on your creative vision and budget.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver high-resolution JPEGs (suitable for print and large-format use) and web-optimized versions. PNG files with transparent backgrounds available for product shots. RAW files can be provided for an additional fee.

Can you match my existing photography style?

Absolutely. Share examples of your existing imagery or inspiration, and we'll match your established aesthetic for brand consistency. We can also help you evolve or refresh your visual style if desired.
