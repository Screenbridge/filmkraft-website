The Proven Explainer Video Formula

Great explainer videos follow a time-tested narrative structure that engages viewers and drives action:

1

Hook (0-5 seconds)

Grab attention immediately with a compelling question, bold statement, or relatable problem. You have seconds to stop the scroll‚Äîmake them count.

Example: "Tired of spending hours on manual data entry?"

2

Problem (5-20 seconds)

Identify the specific pain point your audience faces. Show you understand their frustration. Make them nod along in recognition.

Example: "Most teams waste 10+ hours per week copying data between systems, leading to errors and missed opportunities."

3

Solution (20-45 seconds)

Introduce your product as the answer. Explain what it is and why it's uniquely suited to solve their problem. This is your value proposition.

Example: "That's why we built AutoSync‚Äîan AI-powered tool that automatically syncs data across all your platforms in real-time."

4

How It Works (45-75 seconds)

Show the product in action with 3-4 simple steps. Avoid feature overload‚Äîfocus on the core workflow that delivers value.

Example: "Step 1: Connect your tools. Step 2: Set your sync rules. Step 3: Watch data flow automatically."

5

Benefits & Proof (75-85 seconds)

Highlight the transformation they'll experience. Use specific results, customer testimonials, or impressive metrics.

Example: "Join 10,000+ teams who've cut data entry time by 90% and eliminated costly errors."

6

Call-to-Action (85-90 seconds)

Tell viewers exactly what to do next. Clear, specific, and easy to remember. Create urgency when appropriate.

Example: "Start your free 14-day trial at AutoSync.com‚Äîno credit card required."

Explainer Video FAQs

How long should an explainer video be?

Ideal length is 60-90 seconds. This gives enough time to explain your product without losing attention. Complex products may warrant 2 minutes, but shorter is generally better. Every second must earn its place.

How much does an explainer video cost?

Professional explainer videos typically range from $6,000 (simple whiteboard animation) to $25,000+ (high-end custom animation or live-action). Most quality animated explainers fall in the $8,000-$15,000 range for 60-90 seconds.

Should I choose animation or live-action?

Animation works best for abstract concepts, software interfaces, or when you want unlimited creative freedom. Live-action is better for physical products, service businesses, or when human emotion and credibility are important. We can help you decide.

Can you update my explainer video later?

Yes! With animated videos, we can update scenes, revise copy, or refresh visuals for a fraction of the original cost. Live-action is harder to update (requires reshooting) but we can often work around changes with creative editing.

How long does production take?

Typical timeline is 4-6 weeks from kickoff to final delivery. This includes script development (1-2 weeks), storyboarding and voiceover (1 week), animation/filming (2-3 weeks), and revisions. Rush timelines available if needed.

What information do you need from me?

We'll need: product details, target audience information, key features/benefits, brand guidelines, any existing marketing materials, and your goals for the video. Our discovery process helps extract all necessary information through structured conversations.
