Animation Production FAQs

How much does animation cost?

Animation costs vary widely: $2,500-$5,000 for simple motion graphics, $8,000-$15,000 for 2D character animation, and $12,000-$50,000+ for high-end 3D animation. Cost depends on length, complexity, style, and timeline.

How long does animation take to produce?

Typical timeline is 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Simple motion graphics may take 2-3 weeks, while detailed 3D animation or character work can take 8-12 weeks. We can expedite for urgent projects.

Do I need to provide 3D models or CAD files?

It helps but isn't required. CAD files, technical drawings, photos, or even sketches give us a starting point. If you don't have 3D assets, we can create them from scratch based on your specifications.

Can you match my existing brand style?

Absolutely. We can adapt our animation style to match your existing brand guidelines, color palette, typography, and visual language. Share your brand assets and we'll create animations that feel cohesive with your identity.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver video files (MP4, MOV) optimized for various platforms, plus source files (After Effects, Blender, Cinema 4D) if requested. For 3D visualization, we can also provide still renders in various resolutions and formats.

Can animation be updated or edited later?

Yes! That's one of animation's big advantages. We retain all source files, making it easy to update text, colors, products, or voiceover for a fraction of the original production cost.
