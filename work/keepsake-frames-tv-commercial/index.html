Our Approach

01

Strategic Foundation

We started with a deep dive into Keepsake's customer insights, analyzing what drove conversions and what messaging resonated. This informed a script that balanced emotional storytelling with clear product benefits.

02

Authentic Storytelling

Rather than hiring actors, we built the creative around Sam's genuine passion for the product. We crafted talking points that felt natural and coached him to deliver with warmth and authenticity.

03

Cinematic Production

Shot in a bright, modern living room setting with lifestyle product shots, we created a high-end look that elevated the brand while maintaining approachability. Every frame was designed for TV quality.

04

Modular Assets

We captured multiple lengths (:15, :30, :60) and formats optimized for TV, CTV, YouTube, and Meta, ensuring maximum ROI from the production budget.
