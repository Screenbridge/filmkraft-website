Who we are

A modern TV advertising agency.

Filmkraft is a New York–based advertising agency for brands entering or scaling on television. We are a premium, full-service partner specializing in staple TV advertising formats, including explainers, branded direct response, and testimonial-led campaigns. Our work sits at the intersection of strategy, creative, and production.

Why Filmkraft?

Experienced partners for brands scaling on TV

We have over 10 years of experience building full TV and CTV advertising campaigns for brands at every stage of their television journey, from launching a first TV ad to optimizing and scaling long-running campaigns. We design our work modularly, capturing assets that can be adapted into multiple variations across TV and digital. We understand that TV advertising has the power to build brands and drive results at the same time, and our award-winning work is built to operate comfortably at that intersection under real media spend.

How we work

A practical approach to TV advertising.

We take a practical, modular approach to TV advertising, starting with clear strategic alignment around brand and performance goals. From there, we select the most effective format from proven TV staples, including high-concept brand ads, explainers, and testimonial-led campaigns, and develop the creative end to end, from concepts and scripts through production and delivery. Clients are closely involved throughout the process, with regular check-ins and clear communication to ensure brand identity and product nuance are accurately translated into advertising built to run in market and scale.

Our Process

From concept to delivery, we guide you through every stage

Discovery

We learn about your brand, audience, goals, and budget. Understanding your business ensures our creative aligns with your strategy.

Concept

Our creative team develops multiple concept directions with scripts and visual references. You choose the direction that resonates.

Production

We handle all logistics: casting, locations, crew, equipment, and permits. You approve key decisions and we manage the rest.

Post-Production

Expert editing, color grading, motion graphics, and sound design. Multiple revision rounds ensure you're thrilled with the result.

Delivery

Final files delivered in all required formats with technical specs for each platform. Plus raw footage and project files.

Optimization

We provide performance insights and creative recommendations to help you maximize ROI from your content investment.
